Diet plane for Underweight category

Weight Gain Diet Plan

Vegetarian

Breakfast

Eat a hearty breakfast with a good balance of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Morning Snack

Have a protein-rich snack such as Greek yogurt with fruits or nuts.

Lunch

Opt for a well-rounded meal including legumes, whole grains, and plenty of vegetables.

Afternoon Snack

Enjoy a protein shake or a peanut butter sandwich for a quick energy boost.

Dinner

Focus on protein sources like tofu or tempeh, along with healthy carbohydrates and vegetables.

Non-Vegetarian

Breakfast

Eat a hearty breakfast with a good balance of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Morning Snack

Have a protein-rich snack such as Greek yogurt with fruits or nuts.

Lunch

Opt for a well-rounded meal including lean meat, whole grains, and plenty of vegetables.

Afternoon Snack

Enjoy a protein shake or a turkey sandwich for a quick energy boost.

Dinner

Focus on lean protein sources like chicken breast or fish, along with healthy carbohydrates and vegetables.
